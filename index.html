About Me

Gabby is a Full Stack Developer with experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. She obtained her Bachelors degree in Biology from The University of Lynchburg (formerly Lynchburg College). She recently moved from the Seattle area back to Richmond Virginia. Gabby was born and raised in Virginia and enjoys city life, but also loves to get back to the farm during her time off. Gabby's background is in the medical field and most recently as a birth and postpartum doula. She strives to ensure human connection is not lost through technology.
